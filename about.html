About The Artist



I am Autumn, an artist who finds inspiration in the intricacies of human emotion, translating them into evocative portraits that tell captivating stories. With a focus on drawing expressive faces and painting the breathtaking wonders of our planet, I am deeply moved by the transformative power of flight, the allure of exploring history-rich locales, and the innocent creativity found in children's art. Through my work, I aim to evoke a sense of wonder, empathy, and connection with both the natural world and the human experience.
